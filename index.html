WE ARE:

Minimal Gap Apiary a family owned and operated bee keeping apiary in the Blue Ridge mountains of Virginia. Since 2009 we have been gradually increasing our size, and we now operate three seperate locations. We produce and sell honey, bees wax candles, queens, and nucs.

OUR MISSION:

Save the Bees by sustainably increasing the number of bee colonies in the New River Valley. We hope to achive this through education of the public, helping new beekeepers, and increasing the amount of our own colonies.
